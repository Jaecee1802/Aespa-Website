about aespa.

aespa (에스파) is a South Korean girl group formed by SM Entertainment. The name "aespa" combines "æ," representing "Avatar X Experience," and "aspect," symbolizing their concept of experiencing the real and virtual worlds together. The group debuted on November 17, 2020, with their hit single "Black Mamba." aespa is known for their unique storyline, which integrates a futuristic AI concept where the members interact with their virtual avatars, called "æ." This innovative idea has set them apart in the K-pop industry. Since their debut, aespa has achieved massive success, breaking records and receiving numerous awards, including "Rookie of the Year" titles and accolades for their chart-topping tracks like "Next Level," "Savage," and "Girls. Beyond music, aespa's futuristic concept and stunning visuals have gained them global recognition, earning them spots on prestigious stages like Coachella 2022 and collaborations with brands like Givenchy and Chopard. With their captivating sound, groundbreaking concepts, and ever-growing fanbase, aespa continues to redefine the boundaries of K-pop and inspire their fans, known as MY (마이).
